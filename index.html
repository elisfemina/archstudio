About me

Hello, my name is Elis Femina. I am an architect with a higher education degree, and I have been practicing professionally since 2003. My work encompasses projects for cottages, multi-story residential buildings, public and industrial buildings, interior design, landscaping, and the design of small architectural forms and interior objects.

I strive to create projects that reflect the individuality of my clients, combining aesthetics, functionality, energy efficiency, and reliability. My goal is to fully understand the needs of my clients and deliver unique solutions that harmonize with the architectural context and surrounding environment.

When designing interiors, I take into account all the functional and technical aspects of the space, ensuring maximum comfort and a harmonious blend of artistic and structural solutions. I pay special attention to details such as ventilation, lighting, and water supply to fully realize the intended design.

All projects are developed with consideration of current materials and under my personal supervision. You can order a complete project from me — from the building design to the interior and individual objects. I also provide consultations and help you choose the best solutions.

Projects are developed in a BIM environment using Autodesk Revit, ensuring accuracy and consideration of all nuances for future implementation.

Architecture and interior design have a direct impact on our lives. My goal is to create quality projects that will delight and inspire my clients for years to come.
